Cookie Recipe

Below is the complete guide to making delicious cookies, including ingredients, instructions, and tips.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1 cup
Butter 1/2 cup
Egg 1 large
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Baking Powder 1 tsp
Salt 1/4 tsp

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together butter and sugar.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract, and mix well.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until combined.
  6. Roll the dough into small balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden.
  8. Let the cookies cool on a wire rack before serving.

Best Practices and Tips

  • Use room temperature ingredients for better mixing.
  • Chill the dough for 15 minutes to prevent spreading.
  • Experiment with add-ins like chocolate chips or nuts for variety.
  • Always measure ingredients accurately for consistent results.
